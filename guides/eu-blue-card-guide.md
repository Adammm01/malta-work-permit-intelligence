# EU Blue Card Guide for Malta

The EU Blue Card is a work and residence route for highly qualified non-EU professionals who want to work in an EU country, including Malta.

## Who Is It For?

The EU Blue Card is generally intended for highly qualified workers with advanced skills, qualifications, and employment offers that meet eligibility conditions.

## Why It Is Important

It helps attract skilled professionals to Europe and can be relevant for sectors such as:

- Information technology
- Engineering
- Healthcare
- Finance
- Scientific research
