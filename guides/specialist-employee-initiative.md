# Malta Specialist Employee Initiative Guide

The Specialist Employee Initiative is aimed at workers who have specialized skills and qualifications needed by employers in Malta.

## Purpose

The initiative helps employers recruit skilled foreign workers for roles that require specific technical or professional expertise.

## Suitable For

- Skilled technical workers
- Specialized professionals
- Workers with relevant qualifications or strong experience

## Research Areas For This Repository

This repository may track:

- Salary expectations for specialist roles
- Common industries using this route
- Job demand trends
- Skills shortage patterns

Official source: https://identita.gov.mt/
