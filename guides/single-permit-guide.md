# Malta Single Permit Guide

The Malta Single Permit is the main employment route for many non-EU nationals who want to live and work in Malta.

## What Is a Single Permit?

A Single Permit combines:

- Residence permission
- Employment authorization

It allows an eligible third-country national to work for a specific employer in Malta.

## Who Usually Needs It?

A Single Permit is generally required for non-EU nationals who intend to work in Malta for a longer period.

## Common Documents Required

Typical documents may include:

- Valid passport
- Employment contract
- CV or resume
- Qualifications and experience documents
- Passport-size photographs
- Proof of accommodation
- Health insurance, where applicable
- Employer-related documents

## Important Notes

The exact requirements may vary depending on the applicant’s nationality, job type, employer, and current Maltese rules.
