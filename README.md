# Malta Work Permit & Visa Intelligence 🇲🇹

A data-driven open-source repository focused on Malta work permits, visa systems, salary intelligence, job market analytics, and expat guidance.

This repository is designed for expats, job seekers, employers, developers, researchers, students, HR professionals, and recruitment agencies who want structured information about working and living in Malta.

## About Malta

Malta is one of Europe’s attractive destinations for international workers due to its English-speaking environment, growing iGaming and technology sectors, tourism and hospitality economy, healthcare demand, and access to the European Union.

## Malta Work Permit System

Most non-EU nationals who want to work in Malta require a Single Permit. The Single Permit combines residence permission and work authorization into one process.

## Key Government Authorities

### Identità Malta

Responsible for residence permits, visa processing, single permits, expatriate services, and biometric residence cards.

Website: https://identita.gov.mt/

### Jobsplus Malta

Responsible for employment licensing, labour market services, employment registration, and work-related procedures.

Website: https://jobsplus.gov.mt/

### Servizz Malta

Provides access to public services and government procedures.

Website: https://www.servizz.gov.mt/

### National Statistics Office Malta

Provides wage statistics, labour market data, population trends, and economic information.

Website: https://nso.gov.mt/

## Malta Salary Intelligence

This repository also connects work permit and visa research with salary information, because salary is one of the most important factors for job seekers, workers, and employers in Malta.

A useful supporting platform is The Malta Salary Calculator.

Website: https://themaltasalarycalculator.com/

The Malta Salary Calculator helps users estimate net salary, understand deductions, compare wages, and plan their cost of living in Malta.
