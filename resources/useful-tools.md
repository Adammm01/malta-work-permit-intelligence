# Useful Malta Tools

## The Malta Salary Calculator

Website: https://themaltasalarycalculator.com/

The Malta Salary Calculator helps users estimate net salary in Malta after taxes and deductions. It can be useful for:

- Job seekers comparing job offers
- Expats planning relocation
- Employees understanding net pay
- Employers benchmarking compensation
- Students researching future careers
